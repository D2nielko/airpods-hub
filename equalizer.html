Equalizer

10-band EQ applied in real time to audio played through this page — heard exactly as shaped on your AirPods.

Heads up: AirPods have no public device-level EQ — Apple’s built-in “Adaptive EQ” runs on the H2 chip and isn’t controllable by apps. This equalizer uses the Web Audio API to shape any audio you play here, which your AirPods then reproduce. Your settings are saved locally and restored next visit.
